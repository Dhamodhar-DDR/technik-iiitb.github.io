Talk on Swarm Robotics

Prof. Jishnu Keshavan

Swarm robotics uses numerous, autonomous robotics to accomplish a task. Robot swarms coordinate the behaviors of a large number of relatively simple robots in a decentralized manner. Prof. Jishnu Keshavan, Assistant Professor at the IISc, is one of the leading and active researchers in robotics. He has extensive research experience and has published several papers on Dynamics and control, Autonomous vision, Unmanned Systems, and many other topics.

    Join us on a journey across the endless possibilities and applications of swarm robotics as we begin the quest of learning early by listening to his ideas and thoughts.

Starts At: 17th February, 7.15 PM

Ends At: 17th February, 8.00 PM

Talk on Technology for Social Good

Dr. Manohar Swaminathan

The value of technology lies in its application, putting people at the heart of ideas. Synergy presents a talk on “Technology for social good” by Dr. Manohar Swaminathan, a Principal Researcher at Microsoft Research India. Dr. Manohar has co-founded, managed, advised, and angel-funded several technology startups in India. He has worked on numerous successful technological solutions to stirring problems over the years. His current research is in applying tech to diverse areas of disability.

    Join us as we seek to learn and be inspired to make use of technology in novel and creative ways for the greater good of society.

Closing KeyNote

Mr. Uday Hegde

Synergy presents the event's closing keynote by Uday Hegde, one of our very own. Uday Hegde is a First Batch graduate of the IIITB. After graduating in 2000, Uday worked in the corporate world till launching his own company in 2011. Uday is a co-founder of USEReady, a company that helps major companies with data-driven digital transformation. Uday is a graduate of Harvard Business School and Mysore's National Institute of Engineering.
